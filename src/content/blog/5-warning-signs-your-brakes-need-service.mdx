## Brake warning signs to watch for

Brake issues often start with small symptoms that drivers ignore. If you hear squealing, feel vibration, or notice a soft pedal, it is time to have the system inspected.

Booking service early can prevent more costly repairs later and keep your vehicle safe on the road.

## Typical brake symptoms

- Squealing or grinding noises
- A spongy or soft brake pedal
- Vibration when stopping
- A dashboard brake warning light
