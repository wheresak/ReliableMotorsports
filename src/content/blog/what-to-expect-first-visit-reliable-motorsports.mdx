## We're appointment-only, and that's on purpose

Reliable Motorsports doesn't operate as a walk-in shop. Every vehicle is booked in advance, which means your car gets a proper time slot instead of getting squeezed in between other jobs.

If this is your first time booking with us, here's what the process actually looks like.

## The process, start to finish

- Reach out by phone or the contact form and tell us what your vehicle needs, even if you're not sure exactly what's wrong
- We'll confirm a time that works and give you an idea of what to expect for that visit
- Drop your vehicle off at your scheduled time, or discuss it directly if you're waiting with it
- We diagnose or service based on what you told us, and if we find something else worth mentioning, we'll call before doing any additional work
- You get a clear explanation of what was done and why, not just a bill

There's no pressure to approve extra work on the spot. If something can wait, we'll tell you that too.

## What to expect from us specifically

We're not trying to upsell you on services you don't need. If your vehicle needs a repair, we'll explain what's actually going on and why we're recommending it. If it doesn't need something, we'll say that plainly instead of padding the invoice.

Being appointment-only means the shop isn't juggling walk-ins while working on your car, so the attention to detail holds up from the first visit onward.

If you haven't been in yet, [reach out through our contact page](/contact) and we'll get you booked in. Contact us to schedule service in Scarborough.
